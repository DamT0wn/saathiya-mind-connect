Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30th October 2025
Last Updated: 30th October 2025

Welcome to Saathi, your trusted mental wellness companion. We're committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share your information. This policy explains our practices in simple, clear language.

1. Introduction

Saathi (Saathi) operates the website and AI-powered mental wellness platform. We understand that mental health information is deeply personal, and we take extra care to protect your privacy and maintain your trust.

Data Controller: Saathi, New Delhi

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, age range, preferred language
  • Wellness Data: Mood entries, journal entries, goals you set
  • Chat Conversations: Messages you send to our AI chatbot
  • Support Requests: When you contact us for help or feedback
  • Profile Preferences: Settings, notification preferences, accessibility needs

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, IP address (anonymized)
  • Performance Data: App crashes, load times, error logs
  • Analytics: How you interact with our platform to improve user experience

2.3 Information From Third Parties

  • Authentication Services: If you sign in with Google or other providers
  • Crisis Resources: Anonymous referral data from mental health organizations

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Our Service: Deliver personalized AI responses and track your wellness journey
  • Improve Safety: Detect crisis situations and provide appropriate resources
  • Enhance Experience: Personalize content and improve our AI responses
  • Communicate: Send important updates, respond to your questions
  • Research: Improve mental health support (always anonymized)
  • Legal Compliance: Meet our legal obligations and protect user safety

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Essential Cookies: Keep you logged in and remember your preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand how people use our platform
  • Performance Cookies: Monitor and improve site performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may limit some platform features.

5. Sharing & Third Parties

We never sell your personal information. We only share data in these limited situations:

  • Crisis Intervention: If we detect imminent risk of harm, we may share information with emergency services or mental health professionals
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our platform (hosting, analytics, customer support)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: If our company is sold or merged (with your consent where required)

6. Data Security

We protect your information using:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive conversations
  • Secure servers with regular security audits
  • Access controls limiting who can see your data
  • Regular security training for our team
  • Incident response procedures for any security issues

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Universal Rights

Regardless of where you live, you have the right to:

  • Access: See what personal data we have about you
  • Correction: Fix any incorrect information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Stop certain uses of your information

7.2 GDPR Rights (EU/UK/EEA Residents)

If you're in the EU, UK, or EEA, you also have rights to:

  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restrict: Limit how we process your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Remove consent for data processing
  • Lodge Complaints: File complaints with your data protection authority

7.3 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

California residents can:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Delete personal information (with some exceptions)
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

7.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

  • Email us: saathi.hey@gmail.com
  • In-app settings: Some rights can be exercised directly in your account settings

We'll respond to your request within 30 days (or as required by local law).

8. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: While you use our service and for 1 year after last activity
  • Chat Data: 2 years to improve AI responses and ensure safety
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data may be kept longer for research
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be kept longer if required by law

9. Children's Privacy

Saathi is designed for users 14 and older. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 14. If you're under 18, please have a parent or guardian review this policy with you.

If we discover we've collected data from a child under 14, we'll delete it immediately. Parents can contact us to review, delete, or stop further collection of their child's information.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside of India. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant data protection authorities
  • Other appropriate safeguards as required by law

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do:

  • We'll notify you via email or in-app notification
  • We'll post the new policy on our website
  • Significant changes will include a 30-day notice period
  • Your continued use constitutes acceptance of updates

13. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I delete my account and all my data?
A: You can request account deletion by emailing us or using our privacy request form. We'll permanently delete your account and associated data within 30 days, except where we're legally required to retain certain information.
Q: How can I opt out of data collection and tracking?
A: You can adjust your privacy settings in your account dashboard, disable cookies in your browser, and email us to opt out of analytics and marketing communications. Some essential data collection is necessary for the service to function.
Q: What cookies do you use and how do I manage them?
A: We use essential cookies (login, preferences), analytics cookies (usage statistics), and performance cookies (site optimization). You can manage cookies through your browser settings or our cookie preferences center. Blocking essential cookies may limit functionality.
